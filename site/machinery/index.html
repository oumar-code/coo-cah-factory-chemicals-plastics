    Item Specification Quantity
    SIS Platform Triconex Tricon / Hima HIMatrix — SIL 2 rated 1 system
    Safety PLC (TMR — Triple Modular Redundant) SIL 2 capable, IEC 61511 compliant 2 (redundant)
    Safety I/O modules Dedicated, segregated from DCS 4 cabinets
    Pressure safety switches (PSS) SIL 2 rated, extruder barrel / die head 12
    High-temperature safety switches (TSS) SIL 2 rated, per extruder zone 24
    Emergency shutdown valves (ESDV) Fail-safe close, actuated on SIS demand 8
    Emergency stop (E-Stop) network Hard-wired + fieldbus, each machine and zone 30+
    Fire & gas detection system Smoke + heat + toxic gas (VOC) — integrated to SIS 1 system
    Safety relay panels Machine-level safety, IEC 62061 compliant 8

    SIS Functions (Safety Instrumented Functions — SIFs): - ESD on high barrel pressure (> 350 bar trip) - ESD on high melt temperature (> 330 °C trip) - ESD on heater system fault (power loss to barrel zones) - Fire suppression trigger integration - Toxic VOC alarm and ventilation auto-start - Emergency isolation of compressed air headers
