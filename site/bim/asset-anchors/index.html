    BIM Asset Anchors¶

    Factory: Coo-Cah Plastics & Polymers Factory (CCH-PLS)
    Document: Asset Spatial Anchor Register v1.0
    Status: PLANNED — Preliminary Coordinates

    1. Grid Reference System¶

    • Building grid is based on the layout basis in docs/floor-plan.md
    • Grid columns: A–G
    • Grid rows: 1–8
    • Nominal bay module reference: 12 m × 24 m planning grid
    • Coordinates are approximate (X,Y) meters measured from NW site datum

    Grid labels are used for operational readability, while X/Y values support BIM exchange.

    6. Data Quality Note¶

    Coordinates in this register are planning anchors only.
    Final, survey-validated and discipline-coordinated coordinates (including exact XYZ and BIM GUIDs) must be locked at FEED/detailed design and then reissued in revision-controlled BIM exports.
