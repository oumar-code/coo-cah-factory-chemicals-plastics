    Floor Plan & Site Layout¶

    Factory: Coo-Cah Plastics & Polymers Factory (CCH-PLS) Document: Site Layout & Zone Design v1.0 Status: PLANNED — Phase 1 Layout Basis Location: Agbara Industrial Estate, Lagos State (primary) / Sagamu, Ogun State (alternative)

    3. Zone Descriptions¶

    Zone A — Extrusion Hall¶

    Sub-zone Equipment Area (m²) Notes
    A1 — Blown Film 2× blown film lines, winders, corona treaters 700 10 m clear height, overhead crane provision
    A2 — Pipe Extrusion HDPE pipe line, calibration, saw, stacker 500 10 m clear height, long bay for pipe handling
    A3 — Compounding / Regrind Granulators, blenders, regrind loops 200 Dust extraction required

    Zone A Requirements: - Mechanical ventilation: 12 air changes/hour (polymer fume extraction) - Floor: Epoxy-coated concrete, anti-static, 30 kN/m² loading - Overhead crane: 5-tonne SWL, full bay coverage for mould/die changes - Fire class: Ordinary hazard (OH2) — dry powder and CO₂ systems

    Zone B — Moulding Hall¶

    Sub-zone Equipment Area (m²) Notes
    B1 — PET System Preform IM, SBM, dryers, conveyors 400 Cleanroom-adjacent, PET dust control
    B2 — IM Machines 3× IM machines, robots, MTCs 700 Hot runner manifold storage nearby
    B3 — Assembly/Packing Inline inspection, box filling, labelling 300 Connects to Zone D (FG Warehouse)

    Zone B Requirements: - Temperature control: 22–26 °C (mould condensation prevention for cold moulds) - Cleanroom level: ISO Class 8 clean area for PET food-contact production - Chilled water grid: Distributed ring main to each machine - Compressed air drops: Every 6 m bay column

    Zone C — Raw Material Warehouse¶

    Parameter Detail
    Area ~400 m² internal + outdoor silo area
    Racking Drive-in racking, 1,000 kg per pallet position
    Granule silos 4× outdoor covered silos, 5,000–10,000 kg each (PP/PE/PET)
    IBC bays 8× IBC storage positions for additives and masterbatch
    Temperature Ambient (covered, ventilated) — polymer granules not temperature-sensitive
    FIFO management Barcode scanning, MES inventory management

    Zone D — Chemical Hazmat Storage¶

    Parameter Detail
    Area ~200 m²
    Construction Fire-rated masonry, self-closing fire doors (FD60)
    Ventilation Forced extraction, 15 air changes/hour, ATEX rated fans
    Spill containment Bunded floor, 110% largest container capacity
    Contents Solvents (cleaning), lubricants, catalyst solutions, processing aids
    Signage NESREA / NFPA NFPA 704 diamond placards
    Access control Restricted, permit-to-work for entry
    COSHH register All hazardous substances documented with SDS

    Zone E — Utilities Building¶

    Sub-zone Equipment Area (m²)
    E1 — Mechanical utilities Chiller, cooling tower pumps, compressed air 200
    E2 — Electrical switchroom Main LV switchboard, distribution boards, ATS 80
    E3 — BESS room LFP battery racks, PCS, thermal management 100
    E4 — Diesel generator 500 kVA set, 2,000 L day tank, exhaust 80
    E5 — ETP pH neutralisation, OWS, discharge monitoring 150

    Zone F — Control Room & MES¶

    Parameter Detail
    Area ~100 m²
    Location Central, overlooking production halls via glass wall
    Construction Pressurised, HVAC controlled (positive pressure vs. factory floor)
    Contents DCS operator workstations (4), MES terminals, SCADA displays, CCTV feeds
    Raised floor Yes — for cabling management
    Fire protection Clean agent (FM200) gaseous suppression
    Access Badge access control

    Zone G — QC Laboratory¶

    Parameter Detail
    Area ~150 m²
    Location Adjacent to Zone B (Moulding), close to production for rapid sampling
    Sub-zones Instrument room (AC controlled), sample prep area, chemical analysis area
    Sample conditioning ISO 291: 23 °C ± 2 °C, 50% RH ± 5% — climate controlled
    Ventilation Fume cupboard for chemical analysis (AA, IV, solvent-based tests)
    Water supply Deionised water for solution viscometry

    Zone H — Finished Goods Warehouse & Dispatch¶

    Parameter Detail
    Area ~600 m² (Phase 1); ~2,000 m² (full scale)
    Racking Selective pallet racking, 1,000–1,200 kg/position
    Pallet positions ~400 (Phase 1)
    Dock levellers 3× loading docks, 1× drive-in
    Quarantine area Clearly marked hold area for QC-failed or awaiting-release stock
    Packing lines Strapping, shrink-wrap, labelling stations
    Temperature Ambient, weather-protected

    5. Fire & Emergency Layout¶

    Feature Specification
    Fire escape routes Minimum 2 routes from all points, max 45 m travel distance
    Assembly point Designated muster point, south of car park
    Fire hydrant ring main Surrounding building, 100 mm bore, 2-bar minimum
    Hose reels Every 30 m throughout production halls
    Dry powder extinguishers Polymer fire class — at each machine
    CO₂ extinguishers Electrical panels, control room, QC lab
    Automatic fire alarm Heat + smoke detectors, manual call points
    Emergency lighting Battery-backed, all escape routes
    Emergency shower/eyewash At hazmat zone entry and each production zone
    First aid room Adjacent to welfare block, 2× trained first aiders per shift

    6. Environmental Controls¶

    Feature Detail
    Effluent management ETP (Zone E5) treats all process wastewater before discharge
    Emissions VOC extraction on all extruders (ducted to atmosphere via HEPA/activated carbon filters)
    Noise Equipment noise insulated; boundary noise level ≤ 65 dB(A) daytime
    Solid waste Regrind loops minimise scrap; off-spec material to recycling (CCH-PLS-008 feedstock)
    Hazardous waste Segregated collection, licensed contractor disposal, NESREA manifest
    Stormwater Bunded areas, interceptors on drainage to prevent contamination

    Layout to be developed into detailed architectural drawings and structural engineering drawings at FEED (Front End Engineering Design) stage. This document represents the process engineering layout basis.
