**SIS Functions (Safety Instrumented Functions — SIFs):**
- ESD on high barrel pressure (> 350 bar trip)
- ESD on high melt temperature (> 330 °C trip)
- ESD on heater system fault (power loss to barrel zones)
- Fire suppression trigger integration
- Toxic VOC alarm and ventilation auto-start
- Emergency isolation of compressed air headers
