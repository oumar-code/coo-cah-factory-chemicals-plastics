**Factory:** Coo-Cah Plastics & Polymers Factory (CCH-PLS)

**Status:** ACTIVE — owner and evidence visibility established  

---

## 1. Purpose

This dashboard is the evidence-facing companion to the readiness sprint tracker. It shows what
proof is expected, who owns it, and whether the item is closeable now or only after commissioning.

## 4. Evidence Discipline

1. Every evidence item must point to an owning document or handover pack.
2. "Ready now" items should be completed during the two-week sprint.
3. Commissioning-dependent items must have placeholders and approvers defined before the sprint ends.
